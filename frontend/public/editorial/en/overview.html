Project Mandate

The "Open Justice" initiative seeks to transform the judiciary from an opaque black box into a transparent, data-driven institution. By leveraging large language models (LLMs) and specialized legal AI, we extract structured insights from thousands of court records to improve public oversight and internal consistency.
